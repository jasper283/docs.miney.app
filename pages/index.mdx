# Introduction

![miney-app](https://miney.app/miney-app-screenshot.png)

Manage your finances effortlessly with Miney, the minimalist expense tracker designed for easy income and expense tracking. With Miney, you can:

- 💡Quick Bookkeeping : Record your income, expenses and transfers within seconds quickly.

- 💼Multiple Account Management : Manage multiple accounts in one place and summarize your fund situation.

- 🎯Personalized Categories : Create unique categories that meet your financial needs. 

- 📈Chart Statistics : Support line charts and pie charts to analyze your expenditure situation. 

- 🎯Flexible Budget Planning : Plan and set the budget to stay within the target expenditure limit. 

- 🔍Bill Search : Filter and summarize your bills through keywords and filters. 

- 📱Multi-device Data Synchronization : Keep your accounts synchronized anytime and anywhere across multiple devices.
